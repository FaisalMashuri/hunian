Hunian is positioned as a DECISION TOOL, not a listing marketplace. Key distinction: users bring their own candidates from anywhere (Mamikos, OLX, WhatsApp, etc.), Hunian helps them decide between those candidates.

**Why this matters:** Competing on listing volume vs Mamikos is unwinnable with small resources. The battleground Hunian can win is "bottom of funnel" — after shortlisting, before deciding.

**Moat source:** Structured data from the survey and compare process. Every user who uses Hunian for a decision cycle generates preference data no competitor has. This compounds over time into AI recommendation quality.

**Primary metric:** Cycle completion rate — % of active seekers who complete the full input → choose cycle. MVP target: 60%.

**Hero feature:** Compare trade-off forward (not score/ranking). The format shows "what you give up" per candidate, not "who scored highest." This is the feature no competitor has built.

**AI role:** Reduce typing (extraction + normalization), explain scores in natural language. AI does NOT make the decision. Rule-based scoring, not AI scoring.
